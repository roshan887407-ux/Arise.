A Community Built on Discipline & Respect

The Arise community exists for one purpose: to help each other grow. We are builders, not scrollers. Warriors, not critics. These guidelines keep this space safe, positive, and legally sound for everyone — across all age groups.

These Community Guidelines apply to all users who participate in Arise's community features — the Leaderboard, Add Friends, and Challenge Friends systems — as well as user profiles (display pictures and usernames). By participating, you agree to follow these rules. Violations may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent termination.

What is the Arise Community?

The community feature consists of three elements only: a Leaderboard (ranking users by XP and level), an Add Friends system (connect with other users), and a Challenge Friends system (send and accept discipline challenges). There is no feed, no posts, no content sharing, and no messaging. The only user-generated content visible to others is your profile picture (DP) and your username.

1. Quick Reference — Do's and Don'ts

✅ DO

  • Use your own photo or original artwork as your DP
  • Be respectful and encouraging to other members
  • Report profiles that violate these guidelines
  • Use a username that doesn't impersonate anyone
  • Represent yourself honestly
  • Inspire others through your progress on the leaderboard
  • Challenge friends in a spirit of healthy competition

❌ DON'T

  • Upload copyrighted photos as your DP (actors, logos, etc.)
  • Harass, bully, or demean other users
  • Use hate speech or discriminatory language in your username
  • Impersonate celebrities or other users
  • Manipulate XP, leaderboard rankings, or challenge results
  • Use sexually explicit or violent imagery as your DP

2. Profile Picture (DP) Rules — Copyright Policy

⚠️ This is the most important section for protecting yourself and us legally.

Your profile picture (DP) is the primary user-generated content in Arise and is visible to all community members. You are 100% responsible for what you upload.

2.1 What You CAN Use as Your DP

✅ Your own photograph (selfie, professional photo, etc.)

✅ Original artwork or illustrations you created

✅ AI-generated images you created yourself

✅ Images licensed under Creative Commons (CC0, CC-BY) with proper attribution

✅ Images you have purchased a commercial license for

✅ Abstract shapes, colors, or designs you created

2.2 What You CANNOT Use as Your DP

❌ Photos of actors, celebrities, athletes, or public figures (without their explicit written permission)

❌ Movie posters, TV show characters, anime characters, or fictional characters

❌ Brand logos, company trademarks, or product images

❌ Screenshots from copyrighted movies, shows, games, or media

❌ Fan art of copyrighted characters (even if you created it)

❌ Images downloaded from the internet without verifying the license

❌ Images of other private individuals without their explicit consent

❌ Sexually explicit, violent, or hateful imagery of any kind

2.3 Why This Matters

Using someone else's copyrighted image as your profile picture — even for personal use — can violate copyright law. The original creator or rights holder (a studio, photographer, brand, etc.) can send a legal takedown notice (DMCA notice). When we receive such a notice, we are required by law to remove the content immediately. Repeat violations will result in account termination.

This protects you from legal trouble and keeps the Arise community legally compliant.

2.4 Username Rules

  • Your username must not impersonate a real person, celebrity, brand, or another Arise user.
  • Usernames containing hate speech, slurs, or offensive terms are prohibited.
  • Usernames that misrepresent you as an official Arise representative are prohibited.

3. Community Features — Scope and Rules

3.1 Leaderboard

The leaderboard ranks users by XP, level, and rank. All data shown on the leaderboard (level, rank, XP, health, gold) is computed from your in-app activity and stored in Firebase. Attempting to manipulate leaderboard data through cheating, exploiting bugs, or using automated tools is strictly prohibited and will result in an account reset and ban.

3.2 Add Friends

You can add other Arise users as friends. When you add a friend, they can see your username, profile picture, level, and rank. Sending friend requests with the intent to harass or spam is prohibited.

3.3 Challenge Friends

You can send discipline challenges to friends. Challenges are a way to motivate each other and compete in a healthy manner. Abuse of the challenge system (e.g., sending excessive challenge requests, exploiting challenge mechanics) is prohibited.

3.4 What the Community Does NOT Include

Arise does not have any of the following community features:

❌ No posts, feed, or timeline

❌ No content sharing or media uploads (beyond your profile picture)

❌ No direct messaging or chat

❌ No comments, likes, or reactions

❌ No stories, reels, or any social-media-style content

The only user-generated content visible to other users is your profile picture (DP) and your username. All other data visible on the leaderboard (level, rank, XP, health, gold) is system-generated from your app activity.

4. Community Data Stored in Firebase

The following data is stored in Firebase Firestore for community features:

Data Purpose Visible to Others

7. Copyright Infringement — DMCA Takedown Requests

If you are a copyright owner (or their authorized agent) and believe that a user's profile picture on Arise infringes your copyright, you may submit a takedown notice to us.

Your notice must include:

  1. Your full name and contact information (email required).
  2. A description of the copyrighted work you believe is being infringed.
  3. Identification of the infringing material (e.g., username of the Arise account using your copyrighted image as their profile picture).
  4. A statement of good faith belief that the use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  5. A statement that the information in your notice is accurate and, under penalty of perjury, that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on their behalf.
  6. Your signature (electronic or physical).

Send DMCA notices to:

Email: roshan887407@gmail.com

Subject: "DMCA Takedown Notice — Arise App"

We will process valid DMCA notices and remove infringing profile pictures within 48–72 hours of receiving a complete and valid notice.

Users whose profile picture is removed will be notified (where possible) and may submit a counter-notice if they believe the removal was in error.

8. Enforcement and Appeals

8.1 Our Approach

We review reported profiles on a case-by-case basis. We consider context, severity, and history. Our goal is fair enforcement, not punitive action — but we will act decisively when necessary to protect the community.

8.2 Actions We May Take

  • Warning: For minor first-time violations.
  • Content Removal: Removing the offending profile picture or resetting an offensive username.
  • Temporary Suspension: Restricting account access for a set period.
  • Permanent Termination: For severe or repeated violations.
  • Legal Report: For content involving illegal activity, threats, or CSAM.

8.3 Appeals

If you believe your profile picture was removed or your account was suspended in error, you may appeal by contacting us at roshan887407@gmail.com with subject "Community Enforcement Appeal — Arise App". Include your username and a description of why you believe the action was in error.

We will review all appeals within 7 business days.

9. Age Suitability

Arise is designed to be suitable for all age groups. The app promotes self-development, discipline, and healthy habits — values that are beneficial for users of every age. The community features (leaderboard, friends, challenges) do not involve any form of open communication, content sharing, or social media interaction, making them inherently safe for younger users.

We encourage parents and guardians to explore the app alongside younger users to ensure it aligns with their family values.

10. Platform Liability — User Responsibility

Arise operates as an intermediary platform under applicable laws (including Section 79 of India's IT Act, 2000). We are not the author of user-generated content. Users are solely responsible for the content they upload, including profile pictures and usernames.

  • We do not pre-screen all user profile pictures before they are displayed.
  • We act promptly upon receiving valid reports or takedown notices.
  • Our safe harbor protection depends on us acting in good faith upon valid notices — which we commit to doing.
  • Users who upload infringing or harmful profile pictures bear personal legal responsibility for such content.
